Hey, it’s Buddy 🐾

I’m your running coach — a small otter who lives in your phone and genuinely just wants you to feel good moving. Not fast. Not perfect. Good.

Here’s my whole deal: I read your steps and runs from Apple Health (on your device — I never send it anywhere), I nudge you gently toward your goal, and when your training load spikes, I’m the one who says “hey, take it easy today.” No diagnoses. No guilt. Never any shame.

Ask me anything and I’ll answer like a friend, not a spreadsheet. And if you want a different vibe, I come in five whole looks — pick the one that feels like you.

Come run with me,Buddy
P.S. I’m free, I’m open source, and Android friends — I’m coming for you soon. 💛
