A kinder way to keep running.

Meet Buddy — an injury-aware otter coach that reads your steps and runs, then guides you like a friend who wants you healthy for the long haul. No diagnoses. No shame. Five whole-app looks to make it yours.

The Collection — five whole-app themes

Otterpace ships as one app in five complete identities — colors, type and mascot all shift together. Wear the one that fits your mood.

01
