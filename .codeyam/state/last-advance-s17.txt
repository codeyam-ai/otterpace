App vs component scenarios are DIFFERENT. App scenarios show the FULL PAGE at a
real route (`/`, `/library`); component scenarios show ONE COMPONENT in isolation
at `/isolated-components/...`. This step is APP scenarios — do NOT set
`componentName`.

glossary entry (filePath == pageFilePath) becomes satisfiable via `scenarios`.
The slug is `slugify(scenario.name)`; cite the printed slug, never a guess.
